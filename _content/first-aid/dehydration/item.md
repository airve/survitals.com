## Symptoms
- Extra thirst
- Dry mouth, lips, nostrils, skin    
- Dark urine color
- Low urine volume
- Ceased sweating
- Appetite loss
- Flushness
- Fast breathing
- Sunken eyes
- Tearless crying
- Headache
- Confusion
- Dizziness
- Sluggishness
- Collapse
- Nausea/vomiting
- Diarrhea
- Fever
