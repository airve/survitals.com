<h2>Symptoms</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Extra thirst</li>
    <li>Dry mouth, lips, nostrils, skin</li>    
    <li>Dark urine color</li>
    <li>Low urine volume</li>
    <li>Ceased sweating</li>
    <li>Appetite loss</li>
    <li>Flushness</li>
    <li>Fast breathing</li>
    <li>Sunken eyes</li>
    <li>Tearless crying</li>
    <li>Headache</li>
    <li>Confusion</li>
    <li>Dizziness</li>
    <li>Sluggishness</li>
    <li>Collapse</li>
    <li>Nausea/vomiting</li>
    <li>Diarrhea</li>
    <li>Fever</li>
</ul>
